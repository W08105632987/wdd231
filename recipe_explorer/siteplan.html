Site Name

Recipe Explorer

The name "Recipe Explorer" was chosen because it conveys a sense of discovery and adventure in cooking. It appeals to beginners who want to explore the world of cooking without feeling intimidated. The word "Explorer" suggests that users will embark on a culinary journey, discovering new recipes and building their cooking confidence along the way.

Site Purpose

Recipe Explorer is designed to help beginner cooks discover simple, easy-to-follow recipes that build confidence in the kitchen. The website provides a curated collection of recipes with clear instructions, preparation times, and ingredient lists. Users can browse recipes dynamically, save their favorites using local storage, and access helpful cooking tips for beginners. The site also features an interactive contact form where users can share their favorite recipes or ask questions. Our goal is to make cooking accessible, fun, and stress-free for those just starting their culinary journey.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

"I'm a college student who has never cooked before. Where can I find simple recipes that don't require expensive ingredients or complicated techniques?"

Scenario 2

"I only have 30 minutes after work to make dinner. Can I find quick recipes that are still healthy and delicious?"

Scenario 3

"I want to learn basic cooking skills. Does this site offer tips and techniques that will help me improve?"

Color Schema

The following colors have been carefully selected to create a warm, inviting, and appetizing visual experience:

Primary Color

#FF6B35

Usage: Main call-to-action buttons, active navigation links, recipe card borders, hero section accents, and important highlights.

Secondary Color

#4E6C50

Usage: Section headings, navigation bar background, footer background, hover states, and secondary buttons.

Text Color

#333333

Usage: All body text, paragraph content, recipe descriptions, instructions, and general readable content.

Background Color

#FFFFFF

Usage: Main page background, card backgrounds, modal backgrounds, and content containers.

Typography

Bebas Neue

Primary Display Font

This bold, impactful font will be used for the main site logo, hero section titles, page headers, and major headings. Its uppercase styling creates a strong visual presence that captures attention immediately.
